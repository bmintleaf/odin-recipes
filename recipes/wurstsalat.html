Wurstsalat

Super tasteful Wurstsalat, mmh!

Although it's called a salad it doesn't have much in common with the healthy version of it ;P

You'll find this kind of salad in nearly every "Biergarten" or restaurant in the southern german region.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 bunch radish
  • 1 red onion
  • 250g meat sausage
  • 5 pickles
  • 1 pinch salt and pepper
  • 1/4 tsp mustard
  • 30ml vinegar (e.g. herb vinegar)
  • 50ml sunflower oil
  • 1 pinch nutmeg
